Emphasis in Engineering and Entertainment Arts

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Utah with an emphasis in Engineering and Entertainment Arts. The U's E.A.E., or video game design, program has been awarded the #1 video game program in the country by the Princeton Review three of the last five years. We learn the fundamentals of game design, the history of video games, how to work with artists, producers, and other team members. We learn everything computer science majors learn and more.
