BACHELOR'S DEGREE IN COMPUTER SCIENCE

Emphasis in Engineering and Entertainment Arts

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Utah with an emphasis in Engineering and Entertainment Arts. The U's E.A.E., or video game design, program has been awarded the #1 video game program in the country by the Princeton Review three of the last five years. We learn the fundamentals of game design, the history of video games, how to work with artists, producers, and other team members. We learn everything computer science majors learn and more.

GAMECRAFT CLUB MEMBER AND TREASURER

University of Utah Game Development Club

At GameCraft we meet weekly to discuss making games. We have guest speakers from the industry speak to us and faculty members teach us about making games. We have monthly game jams and have built a community of developers and artists at the University of Utah. When I joined, the club was dying and had a bad rep with only 4-5 members. Now we are a thriving community with a Discord server of 100+ people, 30+ average attendance at meetings, and are respected by the school and staff.
